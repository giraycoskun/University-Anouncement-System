2019-2020 Academic Year Spring Term S/U Grade System Preferences

Dear Students,

Due to the global Covid-19 outbreak; our students will be able to prefer to continue with letter grades or to choose S/U grade system, for each courses of which SU credit is different than zero, in the end of 2019-2020 Academic year Spring term.

All undergraduate and graduate students of Sabancı University will be able to benefit from this grade preference system. Preferences can be submitted from Bannerweb through Student & Financial Aid > Student Records > Declaration for S/U Grading System Options between June 5, 2020, 18:00 and June 8, 2020, 13:00.

After the preference period, it will not be possible to make any change in submitted preferences.

Click for detailed information about grading system preferences.

We wish success to all our students.
